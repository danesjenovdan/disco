Call for Speakers

We are looking for short presentations about projects and initiatives related to digital sovereignty and how civil society is actively reimagining the idea and direction of technological progress. We are interested in both finished and ongoing activities.

You are invited to apply with a 15-minute presentation. Those selected will be organised into several panels that will conclude with a moderated debate.

We are covering the registration fee and accommodation for all of our speakers. We also have limited funds available for travel subsidies, which are available to those who might need them.

To apply, please fill out the form below by 31 August 2023. We will notify you about the status of your application no later than 10 September.
