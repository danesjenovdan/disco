GREAT NEWS!

Your country is eligible for the Powered by The Netherlands Scholarship programme.

Powered by The Netherlands Scholarship is a 12-country programme aiming to make participation at DISCO Slovenia 2023 accessible to everyone. If you feel you would benefit from this scholarship, please select the “Yes, I would like to apply!” option below and fill out the questionnaire. We will notify you of the status of your application no later than 15 September 2023.

The scholarship covers your registration fee for the conference and your accommodation in Ljubljana, Slovenia, for the duration of the event. However, as we will need some time to process these applications – and Ljubljana is notoriously overbooked –, please book your accommodation independently, making sure that your provider’s cancellation policy allows you to cancel the booking if your scholarship gets approved.
